## üîç ENFORCEMENT

### Why We Enforce
Protecting our trademarks ensures:
1. **Quality Control:** Users know they're getting authentic Software
2. **Brand Integrity:** Prevents confusion and dilution
3. **Legal Protection:** Maintains our exclusive rights
4. **Customer Trust:** Authentic products, no counterfeits

### How We Enforce

#### Step 1: Monitoring
We actively monitor for:
- Unauthorized domain registrations
- Counterfeit software distribution
- Misleading marketing
- Social media impersonation

#### Step 2: Notice
If we discover unauthorized use:
1. **Cease and Desist Letter:** Formal notice requesting you stop
2. **Opportunity to Comply:** Reasonable time to remedy the violation
3. **Dialogue:** We may offer licensing if use is legitimate but unauthorized

#### Step 3: Legal Action (If Necessary)
If unauthorized use continues:
- **Domain Disputes:** UDRP (Uniform Domain-Name Dispute-Resolution Policy) proceedings
- **Takedown Notices:** DMCA or platform-specific reports
- **Litigation:** Civil lawsuit for trademark infringement
  - Injunctions (court orders to stop use)
  - Monetary damages (actual + statutory)
  - Attorney's fees and court costs

**Damages Can Include:**
- Lost profits
- Defendant's profits from infringement
- Statutory damages (up to $200,000 per infringement if willful)
- Destruction of counterfeit materials
